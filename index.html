A New Model for Global Innovation

My journey began not in a boardroom but in a lab. A PhD in Medical Biochemistry gave me a foundational understanding of health — but the distance from the lab bench to the patient’s bedside defined my mission: to bridge brilliant ideas with real-world impact.

As co-founder of SOUTH AFRICA MAKES, we reimagined healthcare supply chains through local digital manufacturing — a model now studied globally.

“Innovation without inclusion is not progress.” This principle guides my advocacy at the African Union, G20, and BRICS forums — ensuring the Fourth Industrial Revolution benefits all.
