A New Model for Global Innovation

       

My journey started in a lab, but my work is defined by its real-world application. I specialize in turning breakthrough ideas into practical, impactful solutions that work on the ground.

When faced with the challenge of healthcare access, I didn't just write a paper; I co-founded SOUTH AFRICA MAKES. We built a new system from the ground up, using pioneering digital manufacturing to create a localized, context-specific innovation model that serves as a case study for others.

This hands-on experience is what I bring to the table. I believe that true progress happens when technology, policy, and human-centered design come together. My focus is on ensuring the next wave of digital health systems are not only intelligent but inclusive and implementable.
